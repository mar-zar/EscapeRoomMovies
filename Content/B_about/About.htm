About Escape Room Movies

Escape Room Movies is a fun app for movie (and series) lovers.

Your task is to recognize some items specific to the given film and get out of the closed room. Some of the elements will be hidden, so it's worth clicking on closed boxes/cabinets, etc. Find them all to get the necessary hints that will lead you to the exit. The passkey is the title of a movie or series.

To begin with, we have prepared 3 seasons, 8 riddles each, from such genres as animation, Netflix, and HBO Go series, horror films, adventure films, science fiction films, and the like. There will be new films that have just left the cinemas and classics of such directors as Quentin Tarantino or Luc Besson. If you are bored with ordinary quizzes and clich√© questions, replicated formats of "Who Wants to Be a Millionaire" or other quiz shows, we hope that Escape Room Movies will let you experience something fresh and extraordinary.

Escape Room Movies - will you manage to get out of the room?

Try putting together all the puzzle pieces and make your best guess. Remember that some of the elements are not that obvious and do not reflect 100% of those from the movie - therefore, you will often need to rack your brain.
For ease of use (if you are not a filmweb trouper), each level offers 3 types of hints: quick answer, erase some unnecessary letters from the keyboard, and one-letter hint. However, do not use them too often so as not to spoil the fun.

Being maniacs of movies and series, we have made every effort to make the game as captivating as possible. What rooms should we add? Leave a review in the store and we will do our best to create them.
