Getting Started

You can download Escape Room Movies on Google Play or App Store.

Google Play

Enter Google Play to download the app.

Click the green Install button to start.
Wait until installation is complete.

Once it's ready, the Play button will turn green. Click on it to launch the game.

App Store

Enter App Store to download the app.

Click blue GET button to start.
Wait until installation is complete.

Once it's ready, the game will launch.

 

Escape Room Movies Compatibility:
iPhone: Requires iOS 8.0 or later
iPad: Requires iPadOS 8.0 or later
iPod touch: Requires iOS 8.0 or later
Mac:Requires macOS 11.0 or later and a Mac with Apple M1 chip or later
